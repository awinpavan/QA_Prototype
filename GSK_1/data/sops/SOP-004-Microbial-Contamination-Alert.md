# SOP-003: Microbial Contamination Alert

**Description:** This SOP details the immediate actions required following a positive microbial contamination result from an in-process sample or environmental monitoring plate.

**Action Steps:**
1.  Halt production for the affected line or cleanroom area immediately.
2.  Place the entire in-process batch on "Quality Hold" status in the inventory system.
3.  Notify the Microbiology QC Lead and the Head of Quality Assurance.
4.  Initiate an investigation to identify the source of the contamination. This includes re-sampling, checking personnel logs, and reviewing recent sanitation records.
5.  Production cannot resume until the investigation is complete and a CAPA plan is approved.
