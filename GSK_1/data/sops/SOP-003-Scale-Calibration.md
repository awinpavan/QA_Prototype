# Title: SOP-003: Weekly Calibration of Digital Scales

**Description:** To ensure measurement accuracy, all digital scales in the formulation area must be calibrated at the beginning of the first shift every Monday.

**Action Steps:**
1. Ensure the scale is on a level surface and free of debris.
2. Power on the scale and allow it to warm up for 60 seconds.
3. Use the certified 100g and 500g calibration weights.
4. Place the 100g weight on the scale and verify the reading is within +/- 0.01g. Record the value.
5. Place the 500g weight on the scale and verify the reading is within +/- 0.05g. Record the value.
6. If any reading is outside the tolerance, tag the equipment as "Out of Service" and notify the lab manager.
