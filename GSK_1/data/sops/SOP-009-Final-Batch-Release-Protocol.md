# SOP-009: Final Batch Release Protocol

**Description:** This SOP outlines the final checklist and requirements that must be met before a batch of finished product is released for commercial distribution.

**Action Steps:**
1.  Confirm that the Batch Manufacturing Record (BMR) has been fully reviewed and approved by QA (Reference SOP-004).
2.  Verify that all required QC lab tests (e.g., purity, potency, dissolution) have been completed and meet all specifications.
3.  Ensure that any and all deviation reports associated with the batch have been fully investigated and closed.
4.  Check that environmental monitoring data for the production run is within acceptable limits.
5.  Once all conditions are met, the authorized QA Manager signs the official "Certificate of Release," and the product status is changed to "Released" in the inventory system.
