# SOP-002: Pressure Anomaly Response

**Description:** This procedure details the required steps when reactor vessel pressure exceeds 150 PSI during the mixing phase. This is considered a critical deviation.

**Action Steps:**
1. Do not attempt to manually vent the system.
2. Immediately trigger the E-STOP for the affected production line.
3. Alert the floor supervisor and the on-site engineering team via radio.
4. Evacuate the immediate area to a minimum safe distance of 15 meters.
5. Await clearance from the engineering team before re-entering the area.
