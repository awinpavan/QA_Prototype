# SOP-001: Temperature Deviation Handling

**Description:** This document outlines the standard operating procedure for addressing temperature deviations of more than 5% from the target during active batch processing.

**Action Steps:**
1. Immediately notify the shift supervisor.
2. Record the deviation event in the batch log with a timestamp and operator ID.
3. Isolate the affected batch for quality review.
4. Initiate a Corrective and Preventive Action (CAPA) report within 24 hours.
