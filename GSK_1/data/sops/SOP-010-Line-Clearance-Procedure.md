# SOP-010: Line Clearance Procedure

**Description:** This procedure ensures that a production area and all associated equipment are free from materials, documents, and products from the previous batch before a new batch is started. This is critical for preventing mix-ups.

**Action Steps:**
1.  At the conclusion of a batch run, remove all unused materials, labels, and finished products from the production line.
2.  Perform the required cleaning as per SOP-002: Equipment Cleaning and Sanitization.   
3.  An operator must conduct a full visual inspection of the area and equipment to confirm clearance. 
4.  A second, independent check must be performed by a different operator or a QA representative.
5.  Both individuals must sign the "Line Clearance" section in the new batch record, confirming the area is ready for the next operation.
