# SOP-007: Unscheduled Equipment Downtime

**Description:** This SOP covers the documentation and response procedures for any unscheduled equipment failure or downtime that lasts longer than 15 minutes.

**Action Steps:**
1.  Immediately notify the shift supervisor and the Maintenance department with the equipment ID and a description of the failure.
2.  If the equipment contains in-process material, assess and document any potential impact on the batch (e.g., temperature change, settling of suspension).
3.  Create a "Maintenance Request" in the system, noting the time of failure.
4.  Once maintenance is complete, the Maintenance engineer must complete a "Return to Service" form.
5.  Quality Assurance must review the downtime event and the "Return to Service" form to formally approve the restart of manufacturing operations.
