# SOP-005: Handling of Spills and Hazardous Materials

**Description:**This SOP provides instructions for the safe and compliant handling of chemical or biological spills within the manufacturing facility.

**Action Steps**

1. Immediately alert all personnel in the vicinity of the spill.
2. If the material is hazardous, evacuate the immediate area.
3. Notify the Environmental Health & Safety (EHS) officer and the shift supervisor.
4. Consult the Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) for the spilled substance to determine appropriate personal protective equipment (PPE) and cleanup procedure.
5. Use the designated spill containment kit to absorb and neutralize the material. Dispose of contaminated materials in the marked hazardous waste containers.
