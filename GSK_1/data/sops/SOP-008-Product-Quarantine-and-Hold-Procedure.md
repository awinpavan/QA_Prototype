# SOP-008: Product Quarantine and Hold Procedure

**Description:** This document describes the process for placing finished products, raw materials, or in-process goods into quarantine or on quality hold due to a suspected defect or deviation.

**Action Steps:**
1.  Identify the full scope of the material to be held (e.g., specific lot numbers, date ranges).
2.  Change the status of the material in the inventory management system (e.g., SAP) to "QI Hold - Restricted."
3.  If the material is physical, move it to the designated, access-controlled quarantine cage or area.
4.  Affix highly visible "QUARANTINE - DO NOT USE" labels to all pallets or containers.
5.  The material cannot be moved, used, or shipped until a formal disposition and release is authorized in writing by the Head of Quality.
